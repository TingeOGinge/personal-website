Introducing: Myself

Well, thank you for visiting my humble little page. My name is Dillon O’Shea and I’m currently studying Computer Science on an integrated Masters course.

I joined university at the relatively ripe old age of 23, having worked in a restaurant until I was 20 before spending 3 years working for an outsourcing sales company.

During these roles I gained valuable experience and insight into the professional world, as well develop fundamental soft skills such as effective communication and working as part of a productive team.
